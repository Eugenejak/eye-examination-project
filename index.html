Instructions

For the best accuracy, have someone assist you when testing your vision with this eye chart.

  1. Place the chart on a wall 10 feet away.
  2. Cover one eye with your hand or another item.
  3. Read the letters on the line aloud.
  4. Switch to the other eye and repeat.
